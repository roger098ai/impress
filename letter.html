My dearest Princess,

I wanna say something to you

Hum do alag shahron mein hain, har subah meri aankhen teri muskurahat ki talash mein hoti hain. Tere pyaare chehre ki yaadon se bhari hui har raat, mere sapne bhi teri mulaqat mein hi guzarte hain. Tera saath meri rooh ko ek aaram deta hai, aur har ek yaad tere saath bitayi hui palon ka ehsaas dilata hai ki humari mohabbat ka koi anjaam nahi.

Jab bhi tera naam yaad aata hai, meri dhadkanen tez ho jaati hain aur zindagi ki har ek rangin khooshi tere saath guzarne ki khwahish se bhar jaati hai. Tere saath guzre har lamhe mein, main ek nayi khushbu, ek nayi muskurahat aur ek nayi umang mehsoos karta hoon. Tere bina jeena adhoora sa lagta hai, par tujhmein hi meri poori duniya basi hai.

Tere saath hote hue, har mushkil asaan lagti hai, har dukh sukoon se bhara ho jaata hai. Aur jab hum dobara milenge, yeh dooriyan sirf ek khaas yaad ban jaayengi, ek pyaari si kahani ka ek naya chapter. Jab tak hum ek doosre ke dil mein hain, hamari mohabbat kabhi kam nahi hogi, aur hum hamesha ek doosre ke liye jee lenge.

Meri jaan, jaan lo ki tum mere liye sabse keemti ho, mujhse zyada pyaari ho. Aur chahe hum door kyun na ho, hamara pyaar koi doori nahi jaanta. Har pal, har dhadkan, tum mere saath ho, hamesha aur hamesha.
